- **Yapay Zeka & MLOps**: LLM'ler, AI araclar, MLOps pipeline'lari ve yapay zekanin DevOps'a entegrasyonu
- **Cloud Native**: Kubernetes, container orchestration, service mesh, serverless ve cloud-native mimariler
